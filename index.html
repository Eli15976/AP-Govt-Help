Constitution

The US Constitution is the Supreme Law of the United States. This document contains everything from a person's basic right, to the powers of the federal branches. Currently, there are 7 Articles withing the Constitution, and 27 amendments. The first 10 amendments are called the Bill of Rights (See here).

Bill Of Rights

The Bill of Rights are the first 10 amendments of the Constitution. It specifies the rights that the state and the people have. The federal government is not allowed to make any laws or rules against the Bill of Rights, unless it is necessary and proper for the safety of the US as a whole.
